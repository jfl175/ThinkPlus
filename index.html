The Integrated Programme in Management Aptitude Test (IPMAT) is a test that assesses management aptitude. Various institutes hold their own IPMATs in order to admit students to their 5-year Integrated Management Programme. The IPM course is now offered by five IIMs: IIM Indore, IIM Rohtak, IIM Ranchi, IIM Bodhgaya, and IIM Jammu. According to reports, IIM Amritsar plans to begin their curriculum in 2023. Apart from IIMs, the IPM programme is also offered by IIFT Kokinanda, NALSAR, and Nirma University.

This year, more than 40,000 students are expected to register for IPMAT, with only 600 places available, resulting in a selection ratio of around 1:66. It isn't simple, but with the right approach and assistance, anyone can pass the IPMAT.
